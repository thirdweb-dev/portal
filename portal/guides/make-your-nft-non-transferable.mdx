### Make it non-transferable

Once created, you should see your newly minted NFT on your dashboard. Click on the **permissions** tab. 

![Navigate to permissions tab](/assets/portal/guides/make-your-nft-non-transferable/select-permissions-tab.png)

From the permissions tab, scroll down to the **Transfer** permissions. Click the drop down that says **Transferrable** and change it to **Non-Transferrable.**

After updating, click on **update permissions**. This will prompt a transaction you need to confirm from your wallet.

![Update to non-transferrable](/assets/portal/guides/make-your-nft-non-transferable/update-non-transferrable-permission.png)
